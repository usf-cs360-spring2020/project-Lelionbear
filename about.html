Why I choose this Data

I choose this data because I enjoy watching movies. I came upon the initial idea of this visualization when I went out in SF to search for the filming set for the movie Matrix 4 with actor Keanu Reeves. I wished to know what other movies have been filmed in the city along with what score rating they recieved. Additionally, I wanted to compare the total profit (revenue - budget = profit) each movie gained. Furthermore, I am just curious to see how I can visualize this information with the dataset and API.
