Project Narrative

San Francisco is a city known for its culture and iconic landmarks. This has been a central point of attention for film makers as they take shots of the city for an upcomming movie. By using the "Film Locations in San Francisco" dataset, I will be exploring the number of locations movie directors choose to film at. I can additionally explore the most shot at location in the city followed by the most popular places to film at in the 21st century.
